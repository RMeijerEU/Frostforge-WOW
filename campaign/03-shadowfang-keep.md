# 03 - Shadowfang Keep

## Status

Draft.

## Purpose

Shadowfang Keep should introduce Arugal, the Worgen curse and the danger of desperate magical solutions.

## Intended themes

- The Worgen are not just beasts
- Arugal's choices had consequences
- The keep should feel haunted and tragic
- Both factions should be able to understand why this place matters

## Draft flow

1. Breadcrumb from previous dungeon chapter
2. Travel toward Silverpine Forest
3. Local explanation of the Worgen threat
4. Introduction to Arugal
5. Enter Shadowfang Keep
6. Defeat Arugal
7. Completion and reward
8. Breadcrumb to the next chapter
