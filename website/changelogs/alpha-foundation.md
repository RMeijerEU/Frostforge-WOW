# Frostforge Alpha Foundation

Frostforge is currently in early alpha.

The first focus is building a guided dungeon campaign through Classic Azeroth. Players will follow short story chains between dungeons, receive useful rewards, and use Playerbots to make solo and small-group dungeon play more accessible.

## Current focus

- Starter flow
- Ragefire Chasm campaign chapter
- Playerbot onboarding
- Wailing Caverns / Deadmines transition
- Website and Discord onboarding
- Early tester feedback

## What to expect

This is an alpha project. Bugs, missing polish and unfinished campaign chapters are expected.
