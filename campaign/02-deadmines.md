# 02 - Deadmines

## Status

Draft.

## Purpose

Deadmines should introduce the Defias Brotherhood, Westfall's poverty, Stormwind's neglect and Edwin VanCleef's rebellion.

## Intended themes

- The Defias are criminals, but not random villains
- Westfall shows the cost of political failure
- VanCleef's story should feel personal and grounded
- Deadmines should feel like the climax of the Westfall conflict

## Draft flow

1. Breadcrumb from Ragefire Chasm or Alliance starter flow
2. Travel to Stormwind / Westfall
3. Introduction to Westfall's problems
4. Defias Brotherhood investigation
5. Enter Deadmines
6. Defeat Edwin VanCleef
7. Completion and reward
8. Breadcrumb to the next chapter
