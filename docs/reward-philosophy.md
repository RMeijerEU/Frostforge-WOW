
Reward Philosophy

Frostforge rewards should feel useful, iconic and appropriate for the character.

Goals
Give players meaningful upgrades
Avoid random overpowered items
Avoid giving class-inappropriate rewards
Use iconic items where possible
Keep the campaign fun without trivializing progression
Notes

Class-specific rewards should be reviewed carefully. A warrior should not receive a hunter-only style reward, and casters should have access to appropriate weapons such as staves, daggers or wands where useful.
