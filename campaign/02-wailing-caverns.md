# 02 - Wailing Caverns

## Status

Draft.

## Purpose

Wailing Caverns should introduce the corruption of the Barrens, the Druids of the Fang and the twisted dream influence beneath the caves.

## Intended themes

- The Barrens is harsh but alive
- The Wailing Caverns are not just a monster cave
- The Druids of the Fang represent corrupted restoration
- The Emerald Dream influence should be hinted at early

## Draft flow

1. Breadcrumb from Ragefire Chasm
2. Travel toward the Barrens / Crossroads
3. Local explanation of strange growth and corrupted wildlife
4. Introduction to the Druids of the Fang
5. Enter Wailing Caverns
6. Defeat key threats
7. Completion and reward
8. Breadcrumb to the next chapter
