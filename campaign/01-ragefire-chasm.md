# 01 - Ragefire Chasm

## Status

Implemented / alpha testing.

## Purpose

Ragefire Chasm introduces the Frostforge dungeon campaign flow.

Players start around level 10, receive starter support, learn about Playerbots, form a group and are guided into their first dungeon.

## Flow

1. Starter guidance
2. Starter gear and setup
3. Playerbot explanation
4. Travel or teleport to Ragefire Chasm
5. Pathfinder / guide near entrance
6. Dungeon objective
7. Completion reward
8. Breadcrumb to the next campaign chapter

## Design notes

RFC should be simple, direct and easy to understand. It is the first proof that the Frostforge campaign structure works.
