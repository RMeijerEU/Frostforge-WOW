# Frostforge Testing Guide

## What to test

When testing a dungeon flow, check:

- Can a new player understand where to go?
- Does the quest text explain the story clearly?
- Are NPCs easy to find?
- Do teleport or travel steps work?
- Do Playerbots behave well enough?
- Are rewards useful?
- Does the next breadcrumb quest make sense?

## Bug report format

```text
Character:
Faction:
Level:
Quest/Dungeon:
What happened:
What should happen:
Steps to reproduce:
Screenshot/video:
```

## Feedback format

```text
What felt good?
What was confusing?
Was the reward useful?
Was the lore clear?
Was travel annoying?
Would a new player understand this?
```
