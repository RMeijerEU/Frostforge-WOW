# Frostforge Campaign Overview

Frostforge turns Classic Azeroth into a guided dungeon-based lore campaign.

Players follow short questlines between dungeons. These questlines explain the surrounding lore, introduce important factions and characters, and lead naturally into the next dungeon chapter.

## Design philosophy

- Dungeons should feel like story chapters
- Quest text should be short and readable
- Players should always know where to go next
- Travel should feel meaningful, not annoying
- Rewards should feel iconic and useful
- Playerbots should support solo and small-group play

## Current alpha focus

- Starter flow
- Ragefire Chasm
- Transition into Wailing Caverns / Deadmines
- Playerbot usability
- Clear website and Discord onboarding
